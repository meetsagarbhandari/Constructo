About Us

International Waterproofing is a trusted name in waterproofing solutions, built on a strong foundation of experience, expertise, and integrity. Established in 1988 by Mr. Md. Hussain, the company has proudly served residential, commercial, and industrial clients for over three decades, delivering reliable and long-lasting waterproofing services.

In 2010, the second generation of leadership, Mr. Mohammed Jakir Hussain (B.E. Chemical Engineering), took over the business following his father’s retirement. Backed by a solid technical background and over 14 years of hands-on industry experience, he has independently led and expanded the company, strengthening its reputation as a dependable and professional waterproofing service provider.

Under his leadership, International Waterproofing has successfully combined traditional expertise with modern techniques, ensuring high standards of safety, efficiency, and performance on every project. Today, we are recognized for our commitment to quality workmanship, timely execution, and customer-focused service.
