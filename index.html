Welcome to International Waterproofing

With the guidance of owner Mr Jakir Hussain and equally dedicated team IWP brings a strong profile in the waterproofing field. Our company has experience of over a decade and caters to the construction industry’s and individuals needs for waterproofing solutions.

We provide watertight solutions for complex substructures, concrete roofs, metal roofs, wet areas, expansion joints and any structure where water ingress is a concern. We offer a range of waterproofing solutions to match the exact project specifications.
